The Concept

A shared hub.
Your event inside it.

Common Ground is a week-long pop-up space in Mumbai, timed around Devcon. Partners take over a full day — their speakers, their agenda, their community — inside a shared, locally-owned venue. Infrastructure and costs are split. Everything else is yours.

Based on Mumbai market research for Oct/Nov 2026. F&B is the largest single cost at this scale. Venue pricing reflects Devcon week demand — we're targeting locally-owned creative spaces (e.g. G5A Foundation, Worli) which offer mission-aligned flexibility. A finalised budget will be shared with confirmed partners before commitments are locked in.
