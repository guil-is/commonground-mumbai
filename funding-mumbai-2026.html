What is this

A shared hub.
Your event inside it.

Common Ground is a week-long pop-up space in Mumbai, running the week before Devcon. Partners take over a full day — their speakers, their agenda, their community — inside a shared, locally-owned venue. Infrastructure and costs are split. Everything else is yours.
