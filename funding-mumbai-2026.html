What is Common Ground

Not a conference.
A home base.

Common Ground runs for the full week before Devcon. Open co-working by day, partner-programmed events on schedule, community dinners in the evenings. One locally-owned venue. One shared energy across different communities.

01
Your event, your rules
Day Partners get full creative control over their programming — speakers, format, agenda, attendee experience. You bring the community. We handle the venue, logistics, and infrastructure.
02
Regenerative by design
We don't book hotel ballrooms or convention centres. We find the best locally-owned creative space in Mumbai and direct every euro of event spend there — because that's what regenerative looks like in practice.
03
Shared costs, distinct presence
Partners share venue and infrastructure costs while maintaining their own identity and programme. Premium setup at a fraction of what it would cost to run the same event independently.

Where the
money goes.

We believe partners deserve to see the numbers from the start. Below is our current planning estimate — venue is the biggest variable and will be confirmed once we've secured the right locally-owned space in Mumbai.

Line item Planning estimate
Venue Locally-owned space, 1 week, 150–200 pax — main variable €12,000 – €25,000
A/V & production €6,000 – €12,000
F&B Catering, coffee service, evening events €8,000 – €15,000
Team & coordination Travel, accommodation, on-ground staff €6,000 – €10,000
Design, branding & print €2,000 – €4,000
Contingency (15%) €5,000 – €10,000
Total target budget ~€39,000 – €76,000

These are planning estimates as of early 2026. Venue cost is the single biggest variable and will be confirmed once a locally-owned space is secured. A finalised budget will be shared with all confirmed partners before any commitments are locked in.
