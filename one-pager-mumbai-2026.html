The Concept

One space.
Many communities.

Common Ground is a shared pop-up space co-created by multiple organisations from the Ethereum and Web3 ecosystem. We pool resources to secure a locally-owned venue in Mumbai for a full week — a base for co-working, programming, and community gathering during India Blockchain Week.

No hotel conference rooms. No scattered side events. One rooted space, shared by people who care about the same things.

Co-fund a local space

Pool venue, production, and operational costs with other aligned organisations. A better location at a fraction of what any single partner would pay alone — while keeping it locally owned and values-aligned.

Programme your day

Take over a full day — or a session block — with your speakers, your agenda, your community. Full creative control inside a shared infrastructure. You bring the vision; we handle the space.

Connect across the ecosystem

One hub brings together key organisations from DeFi, ReFi, public goods, climate, governance, AI, and more. Share the week, share the audience, and build relationships that last beyond the conference.
