A collaborative pop-up space co-created by multiple organisations. We pool resources to secure a shared venue in Mumbai the week before Devcon — a base for co-working, programming and gathering for the entire Ethereum community.
Any Ethereum-aligned organisation — protocols, DAOs, public goods projects, local communities, or impact-focused teams. We look for diversity of mission and geography, not just funding capacity.
Your event, your format, your rules — inside a shared, premium venue. Partners get dedicated programming slots with full creative control over their own programming, co-branding across all materials, and a week-long presence at the heart of Devcon week. You bring the community. We handle the infrastructure.
Side events compete. Common Ground Mumbai collaborates. Partners share one central space for the whole week — which means a single, high-quality destination rather than another event on a crowded calendar.
The week before Devcon 2026, in the BKC district of Mumbai — walking distance from the main Devcon venue. Exact dates will be confirmed once the Devcon schedule is published.
