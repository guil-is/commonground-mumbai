The Concept

A shared hub.
Your event inside it.

Common Ground is a week-long pop-up space in Mumbai, co-created by multiple organisations from the Ethereum and Web3 ecosystem. We pool resources to secure a locally-owned venue, a base for co-working, programming, and community gathering during India Blockchain Week.

Regen Village Brussels 2024 · Highlights
Share costs & resources
Pool venue, production, and operational costs with other aligned organisations. Better space, lower per-partner cost. Infrastructure is shared; vision is yours.
A home base for your community
Give your people a place to land. A shared hub means your community has somewhere to gather, rest, and connect all week long, not just during your slot.
Co-fund a locally-owned space
In Brussels we co-funded the Commons Hub, a permanent community space that outlasted the event. We want to do the same in Mumbai, and leave something behind.
